##### Privacy Policy



Last updated: April 18, 2026



##### Overview



Hopper Note is a local desktop application for journaling and working with imported highlight data.



The app is designed to run on your device and store your data locally.



##### What data is stored



Hopper Note may store the following information on your device:



* journal entries
* entry titles
* text formatting
* imported highlights
* notes attached to highlights
* inserted images
* basic app settings and saved state



##### Where data is stored



Your data is stored locally on the device you use to run Hopper Note.



Depending on how the app is running, storage may use:



* local application files created by Electron
* local browser storage used by the app



##### Data sharing



* Hopper Note does not require an account.
* Hopper Note does not upload your entries or highlights to a remote server.
* Hopper Note does not sell or share your personal data.
* Hopper Note does not include advertising or built-in analytics.

##### 

##### Imports and exports



If you import CSV files, the contents are processed locally so the app can display your highlights.



If you export entries or highlights, those files are saved to a location you choose on your device.

##### Third-party content



If you choose to embed content from services such as YouTube or Vimeo, those services may collect information under their own privacy policies when loaded.





###### You are responsible for backing up important data and securing access to your own device.
